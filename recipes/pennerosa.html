Penne Rosa

Description

Penne Rosa is a delicious pasta dish consisting of penne noodles tossed in creamy tomatoe sauce, usually accompanied by various vegetables like mushrooms, spinach, diced tomatoes, and garlic. You can also add your choice of meats to it. My personal favorite is thinly sliced steak cooked all the way through.

My first encounter with penne rosa was in Austin, Texas while working at a restauraunt called Noodles & Company. I've always been a fan of pasta dishes and after trying it the first time it quickly became my favorite food dish. I would order it extra spicy with steak every day I worked, and most of my days off. If you enjoy pasta dishes even a little bit, I think this recipe will blow you away.

Ingredients

    Penne pasta noodles
    Marinara sauce
    Sliced mushrooms
    Garlic, pressed
    Roma tomatoes, diced
    Heavy whipping cream
    Fresh spinach leaves
    Red pepper flakes
    Salt and pepper
    Grated parmesan cheese

Steps

    Step 1. Cook penne pasta according to package directions
    Step 2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook for about 3 minutes, until mushrooms are tender.
    Step 3. Stir in cooked and drained penne pasta, marinara sauce, and crushed red pepper flakes.
    Step 4. Add in fresh diced tomatoes and spinach. Stir to combine and cook until spinach is mostly wilted. Stir in the heavy cream. Add kosher salt and pepper. Heat just until warm.
    Step 5. Serve hot with plenty of fresh parmesan cheese grated on top! This would be delicious served with a side of Garlic Bread and a simple Italian salad for a full Italian feast.
